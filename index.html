Pomodoro Timer

25:00



The Pomodoro Method is a study method where you study for 25 minutes, followed by a 5 minute break. This cycle repeats until you want to finish your study session.

Press the START button to begin the study timer. Once the timer ends, it will automatically prompt you to either start the 5 minute break timer or reset the timer entirely.

The STOP button will pause the timer.

The RESET button will end the timer and set it back to the beginning of the study session.
